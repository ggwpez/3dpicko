About

This is the manual belonging to the colony picker C3PickO it consist of three parts: "User Manual", "Configuration" and "Administration", while the "User Manual" is directed towards students and employees who will be operating it "Configuration" is more targeted to administrators. Lastly "Administration" is intended only for administrators. For better understanding the device it is recommended to read both the "User Manual" and "Configuration". All necessary information for successfully picking colonies and configuring the device will be given in the "User Manual" part. This document is intended as an introduction as well as a reference book. The chapter "First Steps" explains what precondition have to be met for operating the device. The chapter "Example Picking Process" demonstrates how a successful colony picking process is done. In the last chapter "Advanced Settings" all further setting are explained. The contact person is Prof. Dr. Johannes Kabisch.
