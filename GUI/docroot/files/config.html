Einstellungen:
  • Druckereinstellungen
    • Point cut_filament_position_ the xy position, in the coordinate system of the printer, the nozzle needs to move to in order to have the filament cut
    • float filament_extrusion_length_ the length up to which the filament shall be extruded, in millimeter
    • float z_coordinate_extruded_filament_above_plates_ the z coordinate, in the coordinate system of the printer, the extruded filament can be moved above the plates without touching them or anything else, in millimeter
    • float z_coordinate_extruded_filament_inside_source_plate_ the z coordinate, in the coordinate system of the printer, the extruded filament touches a colony on the source plate, in millimeter
    • float z_coordinate_extruded_filament_inside_master_plate_ the z coordinate, in the coordinate system of the printer, the extruded filament touches the culture medium on the master plate, in millimeter
    • float z_coordinate_extruded_filament_inside_goal_plate_ the z coordinate, in the coordinate system of the printer, the extruded filament touches the liqour inside the well of the goal plate, in millimeter
  • Einstellung für die Schablone
    • Point global_origin_of_source_plate_ the origin of the slot/cut-out of the source plate given as a point of the coordinate system of the socket
    • Point global_origin_of_master_plate_ the origin of the slot/cut-out of the master plate given as a point of the coordinate system of the socket
    • Point global_origin_of_goal_plate_ the origin of the slot/cut-out of the goal plate given as a point of the coordinate system of the socket
    • GoalPlateOrientation orientation_of_goal_plate_ the orientation of the goal plate compared to the cutout it is lying in.
      • ???
    • float socket_origin_offset_x_ the offset of the origin of the socket to the origin of the printers coordinate system in x direction
    • float socket_origin_offset_y_ the offset of the origin of the socket to the origin of the printers coordinate system in y direction
  • Einstellungen für Master- und Zielplatte
    • int number_of_rows_ The number of rows the goal plate has.
    • int number_of_columns_ The number of columns the goal plate has.
    • int number_of_wells_ The number of wells the goal plate has.
    • float a1_row_offset_ The offset of the center of the first well A1 to the upper edge of the goal plate.
    • float a1_column_offset_ The offset of the center of the first well A1 to the left edge of the goal plate.
    • float well_spacing_center_to_center_ The distance between the center of a well to the center of any directly adjacent wells.
